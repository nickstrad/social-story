Going to the Dentist

Sometimes I need to visit the dentist. The dentist helps keep my teeth healthy and strong.

When I arrive, I sit in the waiting room. I can bring a favorite toy or book to help me feel calm.

The dentist wears gloves and a mask. This helps keep everyone clean and safe. It is okay if it looks a little different.

I sit in a big chair that can lean back. A bright light helps the dentist see my teeth.

The dentist counts my teeth and cleans them gently. It might feel a little funny, but it does not hurt.

When we are done, I get to pick a sticker. I did a great job taking care of my teeth!
